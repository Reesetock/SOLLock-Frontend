SOLLock SlockFarm

SLockFarm is a yield-farm programmed to take atleast 4% as deposit fee for all of its users . 2 percent of the amount will be spent on buyback and Burn. 1% of the total would be pooled or added to existing pools. Marketing and development will receive 1% of the budget.

SOLLock SlockStake

There will be two type of staking on SlockSwap Flexible & Fixed Staking.

Flexible staking will be having a much lesser APY hence you can withdraw it anytime without penalty Flexible staking earn starts on after 24 hour period when its being deposited.

Fixed staking are great for long term holders because SlockSwap gives users a much greater APY than flexible , But as the name suggest you can only redeem it on a certain period of time Fixed staking earning starts 15 hours after when its deposited redeeming it 1 hour before wont be having a penalty but redeeming it 1 hour after it has been deposited will be having a penalty.

Fixed staking penalties Users who redeemed it early will be paying at least half of all of the earnings and cant stake on the platform for at least 3 days.

SOLLock Smart Contract Audits

Smart Contracts that will be submitted on the SOLLock platform will be automatically tested for possible exploits and vulnerabilities as a result, any Smart Contracts that do not pass our initial inspection will not be allowed to be deployed on our SOLLock platform.
